zulpod Cv

Combination : Cefpodoxime 200mg + Potassium Clavulanate 125mg

Zulpod CV combines the powerful antibiotic Cefpodoxime with Clavulanic Acid, offering a broad spectrum of activity against various bacterial infections. This combination is designed to enhance the efficacy of Cefpodoxime by inhibiting beta-lactamase enzymes, which can render some antibiotics ineffective.

Zulpod CV is a reliable choice for the management of bacterial infections, providing both effectiveness and safety. For more information or to discuss treatment options, please consult your healthcare provider.

ZULFIX 200

Combination : Cefixime 200mg

Zulfix 200 contains Cefixime 200mg a third-generation cephalosporin antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections. It works by inhibiting the growth of bacteria, making it effective in managing infections of the respiratory tract, urinary tract, and otitis media, among others.

This product should be used under medical supervision. Please consult a healthcare professional for detailed information on side effects, dosage, and precautions.

Nice to Meet You

Our Purpose

Every day, our people develop meaningful products, services and innovations that tackle social and environmental barriers – and make better everyday health more accessible to millions of people. It all starts with them and you. By inspiring, stretching and supporting you throughout your career journey, we help you build better too.

We do what’s best for the everyday health of millions by focusing on what matters most, by going beyond what’s expected of us and by keeping it human, always. Driven by our deep desire to make a positive impact on people, we never stop seeking better.

What Drives Us

The Prozen story – Our history in the making

As a standalone company our future is firmly in our hands. From our culture to our brand-led innovations, we’re free to focus on what matters to us: better everyday health with humanity. We’re free to shape our business, our way. Joining us on this journey, you’ll make an immediate impact.

What we aim to achieve

Everyday health is everyone’s right. That is why we work to make it more sustainable, inclusive, and achievable. By pairing trusted science with deep human understanding, we’re changing the future of everyday health for millions of people around the world.

What we offer

Nobody likes to feel like they’re standing still, so we make sure you never do. We’re invested in your progress – and make it possible by:

  • Challenging you with stretch projects that inspire you to do your best work.
  • Encouraging you to go beyond your job title and explore other elements of Haleon.
  • Facilitating open, continuous conversations between you and your managers, mentors and coaches.
  • Providing you with opportunities to update your skills, as well as future-ready training.

But we don’t stop there. We reward you for your energy and effort and provide you with the benefits you need to push for that progress.

Our package includes:

  • A competitive base salary.
  • Annual bonuses that reflect our performance.
  • Health and wellbeing programmes that take care of you, physically and mentally.
  • A pension plan membership and savings programmes.
  • Childcare support.
  • 26 weeks of paid equal parental leave, globally.

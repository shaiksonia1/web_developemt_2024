ZULFIX-200

ZULFIX-200 is a powerful antibiotic used to treat a wide range of bacterial infections. It is indicated for the treatment of conditions such as:

  • Pharyngitis/Tonsillitis
  • Acute Sinusitis
  • Community-Acquired Pneumonia
  • Chronic Bronchitis
  • Urinary Tract Infections
  • Skin and Soft Tissue Infections
  • Acute Otitis Media
  • Typhoid

ZULFIX-O DS

ZULFIX-O DS is a potent combination antibiotic used for the treatment of various infections. It effectively targets:

  • Lower and Upper Respiratory Tract Infections (RTIs)
  • ENT Infections
  • Skin Infections
  • Urethral and Cervical Infections
  • Gonorrhea

This powerful formula provides a strict restriction on infections, making it a global winner in treating both mild and severe bacterial infections with precision and efficiency.
